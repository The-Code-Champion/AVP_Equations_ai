Calculate \(C_D\)

C_D = C_{D_0} + K * C_L^2

Calculate \(K\)

$$K = \frac{C_D - C_{D_0}}{C_L^2}$$

Calculate \(C_L\)

$$C_L = \sqrt{\frac{C_D - C_{D_0}}{K}}$$

$$V = \sqrt{\frac{2 \cdot W}{\rho \cdot S \cdot C_L}}$$
